Quality assurance is monitoring and preventing mistakes and defects during the engineering process to ensure a quality product or result.
In software development, quality assurance is used to ensure that software creation and engineering maintain high-quality standards, processes, and procedures.
The STAR method is an acronym for a four-step process to answering a question where you need to offer an example.
You begin by providing a context for the situation, followed by the task or part you played in the event.
Then you continue to elaborate on the action you took and the final result.
Use a specific example and focus on a positive outcome where you improved the situation or learned a valuable lesson.
